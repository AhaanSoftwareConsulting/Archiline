At ARCHILINE, we believe great design is more than lines on a page — it’s the beginning of something real.

We’re an architectural design company in Melbourne that is dedicated to creating modern, sustainable, and luxury spaces that are both inspiring and achievable, delivering high-end design at affordable prices.

With 15+ years of combined experience in residential planning applications, ARCHILINE has built a strong reputation for guiding projects from concept to approval with clarity and confidence.

Our Architectural
Services

Comprehensive architectural and interior design services tailored to each project’s context and purpose.

Town Planning Permits

We manage the full town planning process from start to finish — preparing the required drawings and documentation, responding to council requests, and guiding your application smoothly through approval.

Dual Occupancy Designs

Maximise your land’s potential with smart dual occupancy designs that balance functionality, privacy, and street appeal — all tailored to council requirements and your budget.

Multi Unit Development

From concept planning to permit approvals, we deliver efficient multi-unit development designs that support strong feasibility, compliance, and a clear pathway to council approval.

Plans and Permits
